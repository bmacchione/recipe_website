PB Oatmeal Chia Muffins

1 cups rolled oats
1 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 TBSP baking powder
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/4-1/2 cup honey or maple syrup
1 cup milk
2 eggs
1/4 cup coconut or olive oil
1 TBSP chia seeds
1/3 cup choc chips


DIRECTIONS:

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
2. Mix oats, flour, salt, baking powder.
3. In a separate bowl mix together maple syrup, milk, eggs, pb, and oil.
4. Combine bowls and mix in chia seeds and choc chips.
5. Bake 15-20 minutes.

NOTES:
Can use gf flour and/or non-dairy milk.
