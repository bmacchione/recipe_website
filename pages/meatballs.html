Meatballs

1-1 1/2 lbs ground pork
1 egg
dried or fresh parsley
1 cup breadcrumbs
salt and pepper


DIRECTIONS:

1. Mix all together. May need slightly more/less breadcrumbs.
2. Form into meatballs.
3. Fry in olive oil over med-low heat turning often for 20-30min. Use enough oil so it comes halfway up the meatballs.
4. Add to sauce!



Tomato Sauce

2 TBSP olive oil
1 28oz can tomato puree
1 small can tomato paste
1 tsp salt
fresh or dried parsley
black pepper


DIRECTIONS:

1. Heat olive oil and black pepper in large pot.
2. Add tomato puree and tomato paste. Let cook for a few minutes.
3. Add 4 paste size cans of water, salt, and parsley.
4. Let simmer uncovered for about 2 hours.
5. Add meatballs!

NOTES:
Can add chili flakes to the oil in the beginning.
