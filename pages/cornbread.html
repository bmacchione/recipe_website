Buttermilk Cornbread

3/4 cup cornmeal
1 cup flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
3/4 tsp salt
1 1/4 cup buttermilk
1 egg
4 TBSP butter melted


DIRECTIONS:

1. Mix together cornmeal, flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
2. In a separate bowl whisk egg into milk.
3. Mix wet and dry together and add butter.
4. Pour into 12 muffin tins and bake for 12-15 minutes at 425 degrees.

NOTES:
Can also bake in a buttered cast iron pan for 20 minutes. Ok to sub some regular milk for buttermilk but definitely tastes best with mostly buttermilk.

SOURCE:
The Art of Simple Food by Alice Waters
