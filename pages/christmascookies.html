Christmas Cookies

1 cup softened butter
3/4 cup sugar
1 egg
2 1/4 cup flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
dash salt
1 tsp almond extract


DIRECTIONS:

1. Cream butter and sugar.
2. Add egg and almond extract and beat well.
3. Add rest of the ingredients and food coloring if using.
4. Refrigerate shaped in 3 cylinders for about 20 min.
5. Fill cookie press, press cookies through, decorate.
6. Bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes.

NOTES:
It's ideal to use the cookie press but can also just make circles.
Extra tasty step: melt chocolate chips and make cookie sandwiches with chocolate in between.
