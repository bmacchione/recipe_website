Pancakes

1 1/2 cups flour
3 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/4+ cups milk
1 egg
2 TBSP melted butter


DIRECTIONS:

1. Mix together flour, baking poweder, and salt.
2. Add milk and mix.
3. Add egg and butter.

NOTES:
Might need slightly more milk or slightly less flour
