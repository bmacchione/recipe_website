Southern Spicy Collards

2+ bunches collards
2 TBSP olive oil
1/2-1 chopped onion
1 tsp red pepper flakes
1-2 cloves garlic
2 cups veggie stock
1 16oz can diced tomatoes (with green chiles)
dash apple cider vinegar (optional)


DIRECTIONS:

1. In a large pot over medium heat sautee onions until almost done.
2. Add garlic and chili flakes and cook 1-2 minutes.
3. Add collards and cook 1 minute.
4. Add veggie stock and diced tomatoes, cover and bring to a simmer.
5. Add apple cider vinegar. Cook for about 40 minutes.

NOTES:
This is much more an outline than an actual recipe. I change it every time I make it, but hopefully this will help me remember what generally goes in it. Also, I'm not sure it needs to be covered the whole time it's simmering.
