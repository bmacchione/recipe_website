Pizza Dough

1 cup lukewarm water
1 tsp sugar
1 envelope active dry yeast
1 tsp salt
2 TBSP olive oil
2 1/2-3 cups flour


DIRECTIONS:

1. Dissolve sugar in water and mix in yeast.
2. Mix in salt and oil.
3. Add 1 cup of flour and mix until batter is smooth.
4. Add rest of flour 1/2 cup at time until can knead dough with hands.
5. Knead dough for 4 minutes.
6. Cover dough with olive oil, place in bowl, cover with a towel, and let rise for 1 hour.
7. Punch down and let rise again for 1/2 hour.
8. Bake with no toppings at 450 degrees for about 10 minutes.
9. Add toppings and lower temp to 400 degrees and continue baking until cheese is melted.

NOTES:
Muir Glen makes a no salt added tomato sauce in a can that is cheap and works great for sauce if you add onion, garlic, chili flakes, salt and pepper.
