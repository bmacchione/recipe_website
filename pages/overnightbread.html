No Knead Overnight Bread

3 cups flour
2 tsp salt
1 tsp active dry yeast
1 1/2 cups room temp water


DIRECTIONS:

1. Mix together flour, salt, and yeast.
2. Add water and mix (dough will be shaggy).
3. Cover and let rise 8-18 hours.
4. Form dough into ball without kneading and let rise for another hour.
5. After dough has risen for 30 minutes preheat oven to 450 degrees and place dutch oven inside.
6. Place dough in dutch oven and cover with lid or aluminum foil. Cook for 30 minutes.
7. Remove lid and bake another 15 minutes or until bread is golden.
8. Allow to cool.
