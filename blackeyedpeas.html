Black-Eyed Peas

2 cups dry black-eyed peas
a few strips of bacon
1/2-1 onion diced
2-3 cups broth
pinch of red pepper flakes


DIRECTIONS:

1. Rinse peas and soak 6 hours or overnight.
2. Drain and rinse when done soaking.
3. Cook bacon in skillet. Set aside and keep about 1-2TBSP grease in pan.
4. Add onions to pan and cook for about 5 minutes.
5. Add garlic and cook a couple more minutes.
6. In large pot add beans, onions, garlic, and broth.
7. Bring to a boil.
8. Reduce heat and simmer for 30+ minutes.
9. Just before done add crumbled bacon and simmer a few more minutes.

NOTES:
Can garnish with parsley and/or hot sauce. Also can sub some water for broth.
